Key Features of 6th Pay Commission
  • • Pay Band System: Introduced grade pay + pay band structure
  • • DA Rate: Started at 0% in 2006, reached 107% by 2015
  • • Minimum Salary: ₹7,000 for lowest grade
  • • HRA: 30% (Class A), 20% (Class B), 10% (Class C)
  • • Annual Increment: 3% of pay band + grade pay
