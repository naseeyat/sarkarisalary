Salary Calculation Components
Basic Pay Components:
Basic Pay + Dearness Allowance (DA) + House Rent Allowance (HRA) + Other Allowances
Current DA Rate (2025):
DA = 53% of Basic Pay (Updated twice yearly)
HRA Calculation:
Metro Cities: 24% | Major Cities: 16% | Other Cities: 8%
Important Notes
  • • Entry Pay: Starting salary for fresh recruits in each level
  • • Annual Increment: 3% of basic pay every year
  • • Promotion: Movement to higher pay levels based on experience
  • • Retirement Age: 60 years for most posts, 62 for certain positions
  • • Pension: 50% of last drawn basic pay + DA
