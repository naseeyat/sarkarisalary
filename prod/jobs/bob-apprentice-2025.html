‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q. Will I get a job after completing the apprenticeship?

A. No. This is only a training program. There is NO job guarantee after completion.

Q. Is NAPS/NATS registration mandatory?

A. Yes. You must register on government apprentice portals (NAPS or NATS) before applying on BoB website.

Q. Can I give the exam from my home?

A. Yes. This is a remote proctored exam. You can take it from home but you need a working webcam and microphone.

Q. What is the local language test?

A. You need to pass a language proficiency test in the official language of the state you select for apprenticeship.

Q. Can I choose my preferred location?

A. Yes. You can select 3 location preferences within your chosen state.

Q. What is the validity of the wait-list?

A. The wait-list is valid for 1 year from the date of result declaration.
