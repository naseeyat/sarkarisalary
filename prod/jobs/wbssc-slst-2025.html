‚úÖ ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA

Educational Qualification:

Post Essential Qualification
Clerk (Group C) Passed School Final/Madhyamik or its equivalent OR old HS in lieu of School Final/Madhyamik or its equivalent on or before the last date of receiving online application
Group D Staff Passed Class VIII from any school recognized or affiliated by Board/Council or equivalent on or before the last date of receiving online application

Age Limit:

  • Minimum Age: 18 years (as on 1st January 2025)
  • Maximum Age: 40 years (as on 1st January 2025)
  • Age Relaxation for SC/ST: 5 years
  • Age Relaxation for OBC: 3 years
  • Age Relaxation for PwBD (SC/ST): 5 + 8 = 13 years
  • Age Relaxation for PwBD (OBC): 3 + 8 = 11 years
  • Age Relaxation for PwBD (General): 8 years

Age relaxation as per Hon'ble Supreme Court order (Civil Appeal 4800/2024) will also be applicable.

Medium of Vacancy:

Candidates must have mandatorily passed the language which is the medium of instruction in the concerned school (Bengali), as first/second/third language subject, at the appropriate level as specified in NTS Rules, 2025.

üìù SELECTION PROCESS

For Clerk (Group C):

  1. Written Examination - Objective type
  2. Evaluation of Academic Performance
  3. Prior Similar Experience
  4. Interview - Including typing-cum-computer proficiency test

For Group D Staff:

  1. Written Examination - Objective type
  2. Prior Similar Experience
  3. Interview

Exam Pattern:

  • Question papers will be set in English and Bengali
  • Candidates can apply under any one medium of instruction and only one Region
  • Syllabus will be uploaded on official website in due course
  • No negative marking for wrong answers
  • Only one correct option carrying one mark out of four options for each question
