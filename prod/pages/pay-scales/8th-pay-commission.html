üîç What Minister Ashwini Vaishnav Said

During his address, Railway Minister Ashwini Vaishnav emphasized the government's commitment to improving the lives of central government employees. "The 8th Pay Commission will ensure that our government employees receive fair compensation that reflects their dedication and service to the nation," he stated.

The minister also highlighted that the new pay commission will consider several factors including:

  • Current economic conditions and inflation rates
  • Comparison with private sector salaries
  • Cost of living in different cities
  • Performance-based incentives
  • Pension reforms for retired employees

üìÖ Implementation Timeline

The 8th Pay Commission implementation will follow these stages:

  1. ‚úÖ 2025: 8th Pay Commission Committee constituted with Justice Ranjana Prakash Desai as Chairman
