Career Overview

The Indian Administrative Service (IAS) offers one of the most prestigious career paths in government service. From entry-level Sub Divisional Magistrate to Secretary-level positions, the journey spans 30+ years with significant salary growth and responsibility expansion.

  • Starting salary: ₹99,000 per month
  • Peak earning potential: ₹2,80,000+ per month
  • 7 major career progression stages
  • Comprehensive benefits package included
  • All-India service with nationwide postings
