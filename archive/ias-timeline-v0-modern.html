IAS Career Progression

Complete journey from SDM to Secretary level

Stage One 0-2 years

Sub Divisional Magistrate

₹99,000 monthly

Entry-level position handling revenue administration, licensing, and sub-division management. First exposure to field administration with direct public interface.

  • Revenue administration and land records
  • Public grievance redressal
  • Licensing and permits
  • Magisterial duties
Stage Two 2-4 years

Assistant Collector

₹1,20,000 monthly

Land acquisition, development programs, district coordination and project management. Building comprehensive administrative experience.

  • Land acquisition projects
  • Development scheme implementation
  • Inter-departmental coordination
  • Project management
Stage Three 4-8 years

District Collector

₹1,60,000 monthly

Head of district administration, law & order coordination, crisis management. Peak field experience role with maximum responsibility.

  • District administration leadership
  • Law and order coordination
  • Disaster management
  • Elections and poll duties
Stage Four 8-12 years

Deputy Secretary

₹1,80,000 monthly

Central government policy work, inter-ministerial coordination, file management. Transition from field to policy making.

  • Central government policy work
  • File management and processing
  • Inter-ministerial coordination
  • Parliamentary questions
Stage Five 12-16 years

Joint Secretary

₹2,00,000 monthly

Policy formulation, parliamentary questions, minister briefings, strategic planning. Senior policy leadership role.

  • Policy formulation and analysis
  • Minister briefings
  • Parliamentary work
  • Strategic planning
Stage Six 16-22 years

Additional Secretary

₹2,40,000 monthly

Senior policy making, cabinet note preparation, high-level negotiations. Pre-secretary leadership responsibilities.

  • Senior policy making
  • Cabinet note preparation
  • High-level negotiations
  • Administrative reforms
Peak Level 22+ years

Secretary to Government

₹2,80,000+ monthly

Ministry head, direct minister interface, policy leadership. Apex administrative position with transformational responsibility.

  • Ministry leadership
  • Cabinet interactions
  • Policy leadership
  • Transformational changes
